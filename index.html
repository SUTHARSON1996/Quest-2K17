About QUEST 2K16

Quest is an enthralling cultural frolic and an inter-school extravaganza which unites students from pre-school to higher secondary, where they can ignite their minds, abilities, artistic sense and provide a path for “change” in the society.

Quest was proposed in the year 1997 to mark the 50th year of our independence. Quest prolongs to augment values and cognizance to the youth through various themes such as

“Patriotism, Environmental awareness, Vision 2020, Water and Energy Conservation, Innovation , Smart Madurai, Village: yesterday, today, tomorrow, Clean India...”

The program consists of several events categorized as cultural events, literary events, skill events, Special events and Fun events based on main theme. Every year this program is organized and conducted by 3rd year students of our college under the guidance of staff members of our College Cultural Association and Final year students.

What is Our Goal?

It invites all young and fresh minds to come with innovative ideas. This event is mainly conducted for the welfare of students and to make them realize who they are, and what their potential is. We assure that the day will be a stepping stone for all inquisitive young minds to achieve greater heights. Under the able guidance and benevolence of the Chairman and Correspondent Thiru Karumuttu T Kannan and spirited leadership of the Principal Dr.V.Abhai Kumar, Quest has been celebrated for 20 years and now it is stepping into its grand 21st year.
