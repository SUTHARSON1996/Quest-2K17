About QUEST 2K17

QUEST is all about being a stage to expose students from preschool to higher-secondary, to let them unleash their creative, innovative and inspiring aspirations.

“No soul is without potential” is an undeniable truth quoted by the eternal patron Swami Vivekananda.


But every sculpture needs a sculptor. Every potential needs an exposure.Quest-an interschool competition offers a cultural frolic and festive platform where young minds are brought into the lime light exclusively.It uplifts competitiveness and a fraternity sense parallely.

Quest was proposed in the year 1997 to mark the 50th year of our independence. Quest prolongs to augment values and cognizance to the youth through various themes such as kalams PAVE, Innovation , Smart Madurai, Village: yesterday, today, tomorrow, Clean India...

Stepping into its 20th successful year of genesis, Quest 2017 embarks on its journey for the search of “smart citizen”, the theme of this year’s fiesta. Aiming for the amelioration of the land of the tricolor, this search for a smart citizen revolves around inspiring young minds with a thirst for creating a super nation. Every good citizen adds to the strength of the nation. But every smart citizen is the strength of the nation.

What is Quest 2K17?

QUEST is an endless journey.

The 20th year TCE Quest journey marks its milestone as “The search for smart citizen”.It’s a journey to find and nurture those smart citizens for a better India and a better tomorrow.

Every good citizen adds to the strength of the nation. A smart citizen is the one who hasfar-sighted vision and lateral thinking and who initiates the plans for the sustainable development of our country by utilizing all of our available resources through his innovative ideas based on our treasure, the true treasure which is knowledge and practices of our culture and heritage. Quest 2K17 emphasizes on finding those young smart citizens from a pool of scores of schools, and does not stop there, it will also nurture the growing minds in all the aspects that will bring out the smart citizen in everyone.
